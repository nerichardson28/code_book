Reflection

Just a quick recap on my goals:
1.)Build a “starter kit” of 5 classroom-ready resources (links, unplugged activities, videos) etc.
2.)Learn how to use offline coding activities for grade 9-12 classes (Computer sciences classes)
3.)Develop knowledge of python
4.)Develop a game..start to finish..using scratch
Looking back on my goals, it safe to say that I have a good start for them all as I do have a few ideas
for offline coding activities based on the ones we learned in class.
I also developed a bit of a knowledge of python and will be going back to develop it further.
I also have a starter kit started for resources for using in classes.. most of these have to do with Scratch though as I seem to have spent the most time working on that program.
This brings me to my last goal of developing a game from start to finish, I feel most satisfied with this one as I created
not one but two artificats using scratch and have them both posted below!
I will likely be doing more of those kinds of things in scratch as they are so much fun to make!
All in all, I think I have given myself a good basis for my (hopeful) career in teaching coding to kids.
